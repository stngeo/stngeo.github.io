Web Developer living in London.

I've been working in the IT industry for the last five years. Web Development for me has always been something tempting and seemed unobtainable when I seriously thought about it.

Having participated in a few online events organized by an American author, coach, motivational speaker, and philanthropist Tony Robbins, I realized that our life is too short to miss our opportunities. Tony is AMAZING! My biggest takeaways from him are: If I feel I can't do it, I MUST do it and HAPPINESS equals PROGRESS.

One day I was lucky enough to stumble across Frontend Masters resource. I truly admired it, and I felt that this was the right tool to hit my goal. It is still an early stage of my path to my dream, and I'm bold enough to learn how to code in the evenings and work full-time during the day.

The WORK page shows that I have a proven track record of completed projects, and progress is my strong suit.
